# Goshen Wiki

*An operator's wiki for Christofuturist villages. Live at [goshen.wiki](https://goshen.wiki) (public, noindex).*

## What this is

A trunk knowledge graph for the Christofuturist village blueprint. Built for **Christoindustrialist operators-in-waiting**, people called to start or join a Christ-aligned town of the future. Not a tourist wiki. The voice runs hot.

The seed essay is Gary Sheng's [Refound America manifesto](https://substack.garysheng.com/p/god-calls-on-you-to-refound-america) (July 4, 2025; globalized August 2025). The upstream principles live on [FaithWalk OS](https://faithwalk.garysheng.com) (gated). Goshen owns the applied / town-building / playbook layer.

## How it grows

Goshen grows through the `goshen-field-notes` global skill. Anything inspiring (a YouTube video on intentional communities, an article on Japanese akiya, a transcript with Apostle Delmar, a podcast on gospel music industry) gets processed into an `Inspirations/` entry. The strongest lifts graduate into `Concepts/`, `Case Studies/`, or `Christoindustry Playbooks/` pages over time.
