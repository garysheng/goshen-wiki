# Who this wiki is for
*Christoindustrialist operators-in-waiting. Families called to start or join a Christofuturist village. People sensing they are meant for more than the lukewarm modern city.*

---

This wiki is a playbook for operators. It assumes you have already crossed (or are actively crossing) a threshold: you have stopped believing the modern secular city can form your children, your character, or your work into anything Christ recognizes as His. You have stopped trying to retrofit a lukewarm life into a hot one. You are open to leaving.

You don't need to know where you're going yet. You don't need a name for what you'll build. You need the framework, the patterns, the sequence, the case studies, and the failure modes.

If you are one of the following, this wiki is for you:

- **The Christoindustrialist**: you sense a divine assignment around an industry. You feel called to anchor a kingdom enterprise that becomes the gravitational center of a community.
- **The family looking for a village to join**: you do not want to lead, but you want your children to grow up surrounded by other children whose parents share your worldview. You want neighbors. You want a real church embedded in real life, not a Sunday building.
- **The technologist, healer, educator, farmer, builder, or artist**: you have a craft, and you want to deploy it inside a Christ-aligned community rather than a corporate hellhole.
- **The retiree**: you want to spend your final productive decades around values-aligned, optimistic, smart people building something beautiful.
- **The intercessor**: you have been praying for revival and reformation for years and want to know where to direct your prayers operationally.

This wiki is **not** for tourists, philosophical browsers, or critics looking for soundbites to dunk on. The voice runs hot on purpose. If the spiritual warfare framing feels foreign, start with the [Refound America manifesto](./the-refound-america-manifesto), the seed worldview.
