# What is a Christofuturist village?
*The unit of formation in the flood era. A pocket of heaven on earth, anchored on a Christoindustry, led by a Christoindustrialist who has received God's heart for a specific people and place.*

---

We are in the flood period. Not a flood of water this time, but a flood of lies, spiritual darkness, economic devastation, civilizational collapse. The infrastructure our ancestors built and the institutions that held us together are crumbling in real time.

The Christofuturist village is the ark.

It is a real place. Families live there. Businesses operate there. Children grow up there. Worship happens there. It is in the world but not of it, designed so that staying on the narrow path feels natural rather than heroic.

A Christofuturist village is not a master-planned compound dropped onto land by a developer. It grows organically around a **Christoindustrialist**: a leader who has received God's heart for a specific people and place, who starts with available skills serving immediate needs, and who anchors a **Christoindustry**, an unambiguously good engine of human flourishing rather than a factory chasing quarterly profits at the expense of exploitation.

As the Christoindustry grows, the community grows around it. Capital from the industry funds land, infrastructure, schools, a **Christocompound** (the convergence point where worship, food, learning, daycare, healthcare, and community meet). The village develops boundaries that allow it to be salt and light without becoming lukewarm. Eventually it produces fruit visible enough to magnetize other villages.

There is no one Christofuturist village template. A gospel music village in Atlanta evolves differently from a healer-training village in the Texas hill country differently from an immersive-faith-experience village in Orlando. Different leaders, different denominations, different Christocultures. What they share is a foundation: Christ at the center, transformation before infrastructure, fruit measured spiritually before financially.

This wiki is for the operators-in-waiting, the Christoindustrialists who have begun to feel the call but need the playbook to act on it.
