# Christoindustry Playbooks
*Vertical recipes for the industries that fund and form a Christofuturist village. Each playbook is a how-to written by an operator who has shipped the thing.*

---

Empty on purpose. Playbooks ship here once a Christoindustrialist has walked a vertical far enough to document the moves.

The roadmap targets (see [README](https://github.com/garysheng/goshen-wiki#eventual-shape), Christoindustry Playbooks): Kingdom Housing, Gospel Music Industry, Immersive Faith Experiences, Healer Training Centers, Discipleship Infrastructure (open-source), Quantum / Frontier Science Christoindustries, plus stubs for verticals yet to be named.

Until a playbook exists, vertical-specific lifts collect in [Inspirations](/inspirations/) tagged by industry.
